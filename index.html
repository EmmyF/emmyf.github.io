About Me

Education

Emmy is graduating from Rollins College this May with a dual degree in Computer Science and Theatre Arts with an emphasis in Design/Technical Theatre, specifically lighting design. She has experience as an electrician and designer, as well as in stage management. She is also an experienced leader. Emmy has been a performer, lighting designer, and leader in Rollins Improv Players for the last three years.

Upcoming

Emmy is very excited to be the lighting designer for Anastasia: The Musical at the Annie Russell Theatre this April.

She is also performing in shows as part of Rollins Improv Players from January to May.

This May, 4 Dinners, a show about four queer women navigating early adulthood, will be part of The Orlando International Fringe Theatre Festival, and Emmy is thrilled to be lighting desiging and co-writing that production.
